Heronix Talk

Secure on-premise messaging for schools. Connect teachers, administrators, students, and parents with a communication platform that keeps all messages on your servers. No external dependencies, no data leaving your network.

Explore Features Request Demo

Communication Features

Everything Your School Needs to Stay Connected

Direct Messaging

One-on-one conversations between any users in the system. Teachers to parents, admins to staff, or student-to-teacher communication with appropriate permissions.

Group Channels

Create channels for departments, grade levels, clubs, or any group. Organize conversations by topic and keep everyone informed.

Announcements

Broadcast important messages to the entire school, specific grades, or selected groups. Emergency alerts reach everyone instantly.

Smart Notifications

Customizable notification preferences. Users choose how and when they receive alerts based on message priority and sender.

File Sharing

Share documents, images, and files directly within conversations. All attachments stored securely on your local servers.

Message Search

Full-text search across all conversations. Find any message, file, or discussion quickly with powerful search filters.

Why Heronix Talk

Security and Privacy First

Complete Data Sovereignty

Unlike Slack, Teams, or other cloud messaging platforms, all messages in Heronix Talk stay on your district servers. No data ever leaves your network.

Offline-First Design

Works without internet connectivity. Teachers can message each other even during network outages. Messages sync when connectivity returns.

FERPA Compliant

Designed for education from the ground up. Role-based access controls ensure students and parents only see appropriate communications.

Ecosystem Integration

Seamlessly integrates with Heronix SIS, Hub, and other Heronix products. Send messages about specific students, classes, or schedules with context.
