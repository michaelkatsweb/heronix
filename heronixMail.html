Heronix Mail

A district-owned, self-hosted email platform designed for K-12 environments. Provides full data sovereignty, administrative control, and compliance without reliance on external cloud services. Your district, your data, your control.

Explore Features Get Updates

Complete Email Independence for Your District

Heronix Mail delivers complete data sovereignty by hosting all email infrastructure within your district. No external cloud dependencies, no third-party data access.

Designed specifically for K-12 environments, Heronix Mail provides districts with full administrative control over their email infrastructure. Manage student and staff email accounts, enforce security policies, maintain FERPA and COPPA compliance, and ensure all student communication data remains within your organization. Self-hosted on your own servers, Heronix Mail eliminates concerns about external data access and service interruptions.

Why Choose Heronix Mail

Benefits for K-12 Districts

True Data Privacy

Student and staff email data never leaves your district. No external cloud providers accessing communications, scanning content, or using data for advertising. Complete privacy protection under your direct control.

Predictable Costs

One-time licensing with no per-user monthly fees. Eliminate ongoing subscription costs and regain budget predictability with self-hosted infrastructure you own.

Full Administrative Control

Districts maintain complete control over email policies, retention schedules, security settings, and access controls without limitations imposed by external providers.

No Vendor Lock-In

Standard email protocols ensure compatibility and prevent vendor dependency. Your district owns the infrastructure and can migrate or modify systems as needed without external approval.
