Our Vision

Developing Educational Technology That Makes a Difference

Heronix Education Systems LLC is a startup focused on creating innovative software solutions that streamline school operations and enhance the educational experience.

We're building Eduscheduler-Pro, our flagship product designed with an offline-first, air-gapped architecture. This approach ensures uninterrupted workflow and maximum data security. The system combines automated scheduling, attendance tracking, hall pass management, and secure communication in one comprehensive, easy-to-use platform that operates reliably without constant internet connectivity.
