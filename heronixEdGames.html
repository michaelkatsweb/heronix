Make Learning an Adventure

Heronix EdGames works completely offline and respects student privacy. No information is collected or transmitted externally.

Teachers can customize games to suit specific curriculum needs and projects. Our unique approach integrates quizzes and assessments directly into gameplay, enhancing knowledge retention through interactive learning. Teachers and administrators can track student learning progress internally, and questions can be added, removed, or modified as needed to align with your educational objectives.

Why Choose EdGames

Benefits for Students & Educators

Increased Student Engagement

Game-based learning helps increase student engagement and motivation. Interactive gameplay captures attention and maintains interest while reinforcing educational concepts through play.

Enhanced Knowledge Retention

Integrated quizzes within gameplay help reinforce learning and improve knowledge retention. Interactive gameplay with immediate feedback helps students remember concepts through active participation and repetition.

Internal Progress Insights

Teachers can track student performance and learning progress locally within the platform. Internal analytics help educators identify areas where students need support and adjust instruction accordingly, all without external data transmission.

Teacher-Directed Learning

Teachers can customize content difficulty and progression to match each student's learning pace. Flexible game settings allow educators to accommodate different skill levels within the same classroom.
