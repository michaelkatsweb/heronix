Heronix SIS

A comprehensive Student Information System designed with offline-first, air-gapped architecture for maximum security and uninterrupted workflow. Manage student records, enrollment, grades, attendance, and parent information in one powerful platform.

Explore Features Get Updates

Complete School Management, Offline-First

Heronix Scheduling System is engineered for educational institutions that demand reliability, security, and operational independence.

Built with an air-gapped, offline-first architecture, our comprehensive Student Information System (SIS) ensures your school operations continue uninterrupted regardless of internet connectivity. All critical functions—student records management, scheduling, attendance, hall passes, and communication—work seamlessly without requiring constant online access.

Maximum Data Security

Air-gapped architecture protects sensitive student and staff data from external threats.

Zero Downtime Operations

Work continuously without internet dependency or connectivity interruptions.
